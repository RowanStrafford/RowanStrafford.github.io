    • About Myself

    My name is Rowan Strafford and I am a graduate student from Solent University having just recently finished my Computer Games (Software Development) BSc degree.


    My main interest for programming would be in the field of Artificial intelligence where a 3D space is controlled. A project I had in mind involves the minipulation of objects using cameras within an area. I feel my path for achieving this could be taken down either a games route or a software development one.


    I feel confident in myself to produce work to meet a certain standard while remaining open to others opinions and ideas as well as my own. I am hoping my capacity to think of unique routes to solve goals and fix problems aids me in my development.
