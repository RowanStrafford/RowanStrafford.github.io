Big Byte

Summary

Big-Byte is a mobile game developed with Unity. It is available on the Google Play Store and soon to be released onto the IOS App Store. My contribution to the game includes programming, marketing and a small contribution to the artwork.

Releasing the game to the Google Play Store allowed us to connect with this application on a different level. This meant that all the visuals were polished and the game was heavily tested.

Outcomes

  • Understanding of how to balance a game
  • Learnt and accomplished how to make an application enjoyable for the user
  • Implementation of advertisements to gain revenue and continue developing
  • Practice into reducing impact the application has on the processing
